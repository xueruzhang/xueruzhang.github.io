<h3>A note to prospective students</h3>

<p> I am looking for highly motivated students (starting Spring/Autumn 2023)! if you are interested in joining our group, please send me an email with the following information: (1) your CV; (2) a copy of your transcripts; (3) a brief description of your background and research interests. <strong>Please start your email subject with "[Prospective PhD Student]"</strong>. I apologize in advance as I may not be able to reply to every email.</p> 

<p> If you are a B.Sc. or M.Sc. student at OSU interested in my research, please feel free to reach out. We can find a time to meet and discuss potential projects.</p>
